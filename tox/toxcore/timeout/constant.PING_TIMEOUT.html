tox::toxcore::timeout

Constant tox::toxcore::timeout::PING_TIMEOUT [−] [src]

pub const PING_TIMEOUT: u64 = 5

Number of seconds to reach ping timeout. DHT node has to respond to a PingReq in less than PING_TIMEOUT seconds.

Ping requests are used to check whether node is online, so that it could be added to own DhtNode's Kbucket of close nodes.
